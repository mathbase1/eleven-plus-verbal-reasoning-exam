11+ Verbal Reasoning

Before you begin

Each question is worth one mark. For questions with more than one part, every part must be correct. There is no penalty for a wrong answer.

Full exam simulation: a fresh, fixed-length 80-question verbal-reasoning paper is generated before the 50-minute practice timer begins. You may move between questions and flag items to revisit.

Adaptive skills practice: questions are created one at a time. Lock an answer to continue. There is no backtracking because each response can affect the practice band used next.

Untimed learning practice: a fresh 25-question mixed set. After finishing, review every question to see the rule and worked answer.

Important: This tool covers verbal reasoning only. It does not include English comprehension, mathematics or non-verbal/spatial reasoning. It is independent practice, not an official paper or an exact replica of any school, admissions authority or test provider. Formats and timings vary, so check the current requirements for your target school or authority. Scores and adaptive levels are internal practice measures—not age-standardised scores, psychometric assessments, admissions predictions or guarantees. No official content, endorsement or affiliation is claimed.

New here? Start with Untimed learning practice to learn the controls and review the guidance before attempting the timed simulation.
